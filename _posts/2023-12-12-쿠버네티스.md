                <td>Etcd</td>

                <td>스케줄러</td>

              </tr>
              <tr>
                <td rowspan="4">워커 노드</td>
                <td>Kubelet</td>

              </tr>
              <tr>
                <td>Kube proxy</td>

              </tr>
              <tr>
                <td>Docker</td>
                <td>- 컨테이너를 실행하는 컨테이너 런타임</td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
                <td>Pod</td>
